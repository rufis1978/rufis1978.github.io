Game rules
  • Dancing Lanterns is a video slot with 3 features; Dancing Wild, Pick'em bonus and Free Spins Bonus.
  • A 5-reel, 3-row video slot with 12 symbols.
  • 20 win lines (see pay table for more info).
  • A Wild symbol substitutes for any symbol except Scater.
  • A Wild symbol substitutes for the highest possible winning combination according to the pay table.
  • A winning combination pays from left to right.
  • Coinciding wins on multiple combinations are paid out.
  • Winning combinations and pays are made according to the pay table.
  • The highest win per activated win line is paid.
  • Different reel configurations are used, depending on the game mode (main game or any of the bonus modes).
  • Hot zone: Appears on the middle position on last reel both in main game and Flower Power feature.
  • Flower Power: Trigger up to 3 portals on first 4 reels.
  • The theoretical return to the player for this game is 96.08%.
  • A malfunction voids all winnings.
  • Malfunction in gaming hardware/software; all affected bets are refunded.
  • All game states will be saved for 6 months.
  • Game rounds not finished within 90 days will automatically be closed. Any accumulated wins during that game round will be paid out.
  • Autoplay automatically plays the game for selected number of rounds or when any of the advanced autoplay setting criterias are fulfilled.
  • When changing autoplay settings during a game round, all settings will take effect upon completion of the game round or feature.
  • Some autoplay features may be mandatory for some jurisdictions.
  • When playing with Cryptocurrency, unfinished game rounds and states will automatically reset after 24 hours. Any accumulated wins during this round will be paid out.
Paytable
Lantern wild

One or more DANCING LANTERNS may appear random, below each reel, and fly up at one position each spin.

When the reel stops, the DANCING LANTERN lands on symbol, and transforms into a WILD.

If DANCING LANTERN lands on a SCATTER, bonus game still can be triggered.

Wilds that fly up, and will expand when landing on Golden Lantern symbol.

Scater symbol

Collect 3 or more symbols, and get Free Spins Dancing Lanterns feature.

Pick lanterns to collect free spins number, and get massive wins during the game!

3 or more symbols during FREE SPINS BONUS GAME trigger an additional Pick'em bonus game.

Boost feature

Lantern Boost feature allow to play on ExtraBet mode, with more lanterns.

Up to 7 additional DANCING LANTERNS may appear ramdom, and mobe to ramdom positions on any of the five reels.

Random DANCING LANTERNS generated by LANTERNS BOOST feature, does not land on top position of the reel, or on any position, where lantern already exist.
